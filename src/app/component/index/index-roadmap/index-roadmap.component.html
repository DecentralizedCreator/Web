Decentralizing Content to mitigate Censorship

Mainstream Technology platforms such as Facebook, Twitter and Youtube have been heavily moderating content from content creators they believe are spewing hate speech. The problem becomes the perogative of what defines "hate speech", where it's definition has been consistently changing to benefit special interest groups.

With the Gab takedown. We need to be mindful that any specific hosting platform can choose to censor an individual for their own interests. Decentralized Creator is a completely Open Source platform with the goal of not only fostering the first Open Source non custodial Bitcoin Wallet solution. But also becoming an IPFS Content Curation platform in place of platforms like Youtube or Imgur.
