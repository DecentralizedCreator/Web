Patreon with Cryptocurrency

The intention of Decentralized Creator was not only to create a better, more polished version of Patreon. But, a platform that is tied to the least amount of centralized governing systems as possible.

Currently, we provide both custodial and non custodial wallet solutions for Ethereum. In the future, our goal is to provide a free open source non-custodial wallet api for both Bitcoin and Monero.
