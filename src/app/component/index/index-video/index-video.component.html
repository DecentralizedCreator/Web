A platform devoted to free speech

We believe that free speech is a fundamental human right. Decentralized Creator is a platform that intends to mitigate and respond to concerns revolved around censorship.

This platform is completely open source and not tied to any specific governing Technology Company. (See Gab takedown) This way, if anything were ever to happen to this site specifically, you can simply fork the source code and create your own version of Decentralized Creator.
